Healthcare Data Sources for RAG

Selection Criteria
Only authoritative, public healthcare sources are used to ensure reliability.

Primary Sources
- Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)
- World Health Organization (WHO)
- National Institutes of Health (NIH)

Usage Scope
- General symptom information
- Preventive health guidance
- Indicators for seeking professional care

Exclusions
- No patient-specific medical records
- No diagnostic or treatment protocols
