Few-Shot vs Zero-Shot Prompting in Healthcare

Zero-Shot Prompting
Provides general responses without examples.
Flexible but less controlled.

Few-Shot Prompting
Uses curated examples to demonstrate safe and responsible responses.
Improves consistency and reduces hallucinations.

Comparison
Few-shot prompting is preferred for healthcare scenarios due to safety requirements.

Conclusion
Controlled prompting strategies are critical in high stakes domains.
